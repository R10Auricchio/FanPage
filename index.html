Brief Biography

Harry Styles was very young when he started his musical career. He had had other plans in his life besides being a music artist but once he was constantly told he was a good singer, he decided to audition in X factor. To his luck he managed to be voted up and move on the the next phase. Unfortunately he did not pass the finalist round but later formed a group called One Direction.

How did I learn of him

The first time I heard of Harry Styles was when I listened to his Adore You song. After watching the music video I was curious to know more about him so I did a google search on him. His style and the clothes he wears really interested me and made me envious of how he could wear such crazy outfits and not worry about what other people think. One day I would like to meet him.
